Albert Einstein

About Me

Hi, I’m Albert Einstein, a theoretical physicist born in Ulm, Germany in 1879. My passion is unraveling the universe's mysteries, from relativity to the photoelectric effect. Imagination, to me, surpasses knowledge in driving discovery.

I've contributed ideas like E=mc², expanding our cosmic understanding and advancing technology. My life mantra? “Life is like riding a bicycle. To keep your balance, you must keep moving.” Beyond science, I cherish curiosity and value over success. Welcome to my corner of the digital cosmos!
